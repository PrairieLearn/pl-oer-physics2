Two identical spheres with radius $R$ are separated by distance $d \gg R$: sphere $A$ is charged to ${{params.Q1}}$ and the sphere $B$ charged to ${{params.Q2}}$. The magnitude of the force of attraction on sphere $A$ is $F$. The two spheres are made to touch and are separated again by the same distance $d$.

Determine the expression for the magnitude of the force of attraction on sphere $B$ as a function of $F$.
