Two point charges $q_1={{params.q1}}\rm\ \mu C$ and $q_2={{params.q2}}\rm\ \mu C$ are arranged along the $x$-axis of a coordinate system such that $E=0$ at the origin.

For this problem take $x_1$ as the position of $q_1$ and $x_2$ as the position of $q_2$. Assume that $x_1=1.0\rm\ m$. Find the position of $q_2$.
