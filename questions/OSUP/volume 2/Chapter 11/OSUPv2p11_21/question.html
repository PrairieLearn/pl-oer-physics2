Aircrafts sometimes acquire small static charges. Suppose a supersonic jet has a ${{params.q}}\rm\ {\mu C}$ charge and flies due west at a speed of ${{params.v}}\rm\ m/s$ over Earths south magnetic pole, where the $8 \times 10^{-5}\rm\ T$ magnetic field points straight down into the ground. What is the magnitude of the magnetic force on the plane?
